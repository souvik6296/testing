Event Details

  • Speaker's Talk

    • The Speaker's talk at the techfest in 2024 will undoubtedly be an electrifying highlight of the event. With the theme of the Innovative setting the stage for an unparalleled tech adventure, attendees can expect to be inspired and motivated by the speaker's insights. The talk will likely delve into the importance of innovation, collaboration, and pushing boundaries in the tech industry. Attendees can look forward to gaining valuable knowledge and advice from the speaker, further enhancing their experience at the techfest. It promises to be a captivating session that will ignite the spark of creativity and drive among all participants.

  • Innovative Experience

    • Tech Fest 2024 promises an innovative experience like no other. Dive into a world where creativity and technology collide to create groundbreaking solutions and inspire collaborative success. Experience a myriad of workshops, coding challenges, and interactive sessions that will redefine the way you see technology. Join us in celebrating the art of innovation as we push the boundaries of what is possible. Get ready to be amazed and empowered at Tech Fest 2024!

  • National Level Hackathon

    • Join us for the most anticipated event of Tech Fest 2024 – the Hackathon! This electrifying competition will bring together brilliant minds from universities and colleges across the region to innovate, create, and solve real-world problems. The Hackathon is a high-energy, fast-paced event where teams of students will collaborate to design, develop, and present innovative solutions to pressing challenges. Participants will have the opportunity to showcase their technical skills, creativity, and teamwork in a supportive and competitive environment.

  • Strategic CTFs

    • Gear up for an adrenaline-pumping event at Tech Fest 2024 – the Capture the Flag (CTF) competition! This exciting challenge will bring together sharp minds from universities and colleges to test their cybersecurity skills in a dynamic and competitive environment. The CTF is a high-stakes cybersecurity competition where participants must find and exploit vulnerabilities in a controlled environment. Teams will engage in a series of challenges designed to test their knowledge, skills, and creativity in cybersecurity. This event promises to be a thrilling experience for all involved.
